About Me

My research revolves around the intersection between machine learning and statistics. My recent research focuses on the design and analysis of deep learning models and algorithms, such as diffusion models and algorithm unrolling networks. Other topics that interest me include multi-armed bandit, change-point detection, Bayesian inference, and Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC).
